pageRank: 60E

pageRank can be improved by using a site-map. the number of links, and keywords and titles and pictures to see where the page should rank.

  • A website has a maximum amout of PageRank that is distributed between its pages by internal links
  • The maximum amount of PageRank in a site increases as the number of pages in the site increases.
  • By linking poorly, it is possible to fail to reach the sit's maximum PageRank, but it is not possible to exceed it.

a page within a website that is linked to most often will have the highest pageRank. if all pages link to all other pages the website will have its highest possible page ranking. If there are links that are missed the pageRanking will subsequently not be as high as it could possibly be.
